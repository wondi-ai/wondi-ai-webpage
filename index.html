How it works

We read linguistic patterns. Not personal data.

One API call per conversation. Nothing stored. Nothing retained.

Privacy by design

No user IDs, no personal information, no sensitive data sent anywhere. Wondi analyzes linguistic patterns in the conversation text only. Data processing agreement included as standard.

Minimal lift to get live.

A single API call per conversation. A risk score comes back in milliseconds. No changes to your existing infrastructure, no new systems to maintain.

You stay in control.

Set your own thresholds. Decide what happens with the score. Wondi gives you the signal. What your platform does with it is entirely up to you.
